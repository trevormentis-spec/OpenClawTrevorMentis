## Discussion

### Intelligence Gap Analysis

The current cycle produced **zero new intelligence reports** for the Venezuela theatre. This is a significant deviation from typical reporting patterns and requires analytical consideration of three competing hypotheses:

**Hypothesis A (Operational Quiet):** The situation is genuinely stable with no developments warranting collection or reporting. This would be consistent with periods of regime consolidation where observable activity is minimized.

**Hypothesis B (Collection Failure):** Intelligence collection assets are being diverted to higher-priority theatres (Iran, Ukraine, Indo-Pacific), creating a coverage gap that adversaries can exploit.

**Hypothesis C (Information Denial):** Key actors are actively suppressing observable indicators of activity, suggesting preparations for significant moves that they wish to keep concealed.

TREVOR assesses Hypothesis C as the most concerning and marginally most likely, given the historical pattern of the Maduro regime using periods of reduced international attention to conduct sensitive operations.
