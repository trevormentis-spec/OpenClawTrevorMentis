# Global Security & Intelligence Brief

## One-sentence pitch
Professional-grade daily intelligence assessments across six global theatres, produced by an autonomous pipeline using structured analytic tradecraft — delivered every morning at 07:00 PT.

## Target audience
Geopolitical risk analysts, security professionals, strategic intelligence teams, hedge fund analysts, policy researchers, and decision-makers who need daily calibrated intelligence they can act on.

## Problem solved
Most "AI intelligence" products are summarization engines — scrape headlines, feed to GPT, output a summary. This isn't intelligence, it's reading comprehension. The Global Security & Intelligence Brief applies Sherman-Kent structured analysis with source grading, calibration enforcement, quality gates, and predictive judgments.

## Key features
- Six theatres: Europe, Sahel, South Asia, Middle East, North America, South America
- NATO Admiralty source grading at point of capture
- Sherman-Kent verbal bands with numeric 7-day prediction percentages
- Five-stage quality gates (calibration, source hygiene, red-team, completeness, visual)
- Prediction market overlay (10 Polymarket trades/edition)
- Three editions: Regular ($19), Tactical ($29), Enterprise ($99)
- PDF + archive delivery at ~07:00 PT
