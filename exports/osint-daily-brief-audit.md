# Landing Page Roast — Daily OSINT Briefing

## Overall Score: 4/10

## Top 5 Blockers (ranked by impact)

1. **No real testimonials** — "Satisfied customer 1/2/3" is an empty placeholder. Zero trust signal.
2. **No pricing section** — $19/mo is mentioned but there's no plan structure, no comparison, no "what you get at each tier." 
3. **Generic feature descriptions** — Every feature card says "Feature description." No detail, no specifics.
4. **No audience filter** — "Who is this for?" is never answered. Is it for analysts? Executives? Journalists? Traders?
5. **No sample/example** — No preview of the actual brief. A PDF screenshot or sample excerpt would convert far better.

## Quick Wins (<30 min changes)

- Replace placeholder testimonials with a sample brief excerpt + your own testimonial
- Add specific examples of insights the brief catches (e.g., Strait of Hormuz oil flow disruption)
- Improve CTA: "Subscribe Now" is generic → "Get Your First Brief Free" or "Start Your 14-Day Trial"
- Fix "Feature description" → actual compelling descriptions per feature
- Add a social proof section: "Trusted by analysts at..." or subscriber count or deployment stats
