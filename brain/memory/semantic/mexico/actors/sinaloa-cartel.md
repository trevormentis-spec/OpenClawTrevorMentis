# Entity: Sinaloa Cartel (CDS) — Organizational Overview

**Last updated:** 2026-05-17
**Type:** actor (criminal organization)
**Status:** Active, in internal civil war
**Primary themes:** cartel_security, us_mexico

## Nomenclature

- **Full name:** Cártel de Sinaloa (CDS)
- **Alias:** Sinaloa Cartel, Pacific Cartel, Guzmán-Zambada Organization
- **Factions in conflict:**
  - **Los Chapitos** — led by sons of Joaquín "El Chapo" Guzmán. Iván
    Archivaldo Guzmán Salazar (fugitive), Ovidio Guzmán López (US custody,
    cooperating), Joaquín Guzmán López (US custody, cooperating).
  - **Los Mayitos (La Mayiza)** — led by Ismael Zambada Sicairos (son of
    El Mayo Zambada). The "ranchero" faction, more tactically disciplined.

## Territorial Status (as of May 2026)

**Under Mayiza control:** Majority of Culiacán metro area, Mazatlán, Navolato,
parts of rural Sinaloa.

**Under Chapitos control:** Diminishing — strongholds in Badiraguato
(native region of the Guzmán family), parts of Culiacán.

**Contested daily:** Key neighborhoods and transit corridors across all
three major cities.

**Outside Sinaloa:** CJNG/CDS fronts active in Baja California, Sonora,
Ciudad Juárez, Zacatecas, Chiapas. CDS internal war has weakened their
position against CJNG in these territories.

## Revenue Streams

- Fentanyl production and trafficking (primary — Chapitos specialty)
- Cocaine trafficking (legacy, Mayiza-aligned)
- Methamphetamine production
- Heroin (declining)
- Extortion, kidnapping-for-ransom, vehicle theft
- Huachicoleo (fuel theft) — as documented in Milenio
