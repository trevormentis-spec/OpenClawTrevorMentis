**Score: 5 — Moderate-Significant.** She shows skill at preserving options through calibrated ambiguity (public defiance + quiet cooperation), but Morena's internal faction structure meaningfully constrains her.

**Score: 5 — Moderate-Significant.** She will reshape institutions to reduce constraints but does so through legal/constitutional channels. The judicial reform is the definitive signal — if she pushes through popular election of judges, her D4 may need upward revision to 6.
