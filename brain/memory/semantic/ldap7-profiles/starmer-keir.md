- **May 2026 speech:** Pledged "radical change" — a rhetorical shift toward boldness, but the specific commitments (EU summit, steel nationalisation) remain conditional and unspecified.

**Interaction note:** D1 interacts with D4 (Institutional Constraint Rejection). Starmer's low D1 is partially offset by his respect for institutional process (D4 is moderate-low) — he forecloses options within established frameworks rather than bypassing them.

**Score: 4 — Moderate.** He forecloses options more readily than a high-D1 leader but does so through institutional processes, not arbitrary action.
