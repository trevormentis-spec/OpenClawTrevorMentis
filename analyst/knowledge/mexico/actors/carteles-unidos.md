# Actor: Cárteles Unidos (Michoacán)

**Role:** Cartel coalition — CJNG counterforce in Michoacán
**Leader:** Juan José Farías Álvarez
**Status:** Active — defensive contest with CJNG in Tierra Caliente
**Themes:** cartel_security
**US Designation:** FTO/SDGT (as of 2026)

## Who
A coalition of local and regional criminal groups in Michoacán united by a common enemy (CJNG expansion into their territory), not by shared identity. Includes cells from La Familia Michoacana, Los Viagras, Los Caballeros Templarios remnants, Los Blancos de Troya, and small independent groups like Cártel del Abuelo.

## Key
CU controls the Tepalcatepec municipality (southwest Michoacán, Tierra Caliente) — the strategic stronghold against CJNG expansion. Their territorial strategy is purely defensive: prevent CJNG from taking Tierra Caliente, not expand into CJNG territory. They use IEDs against military patrols (a factor in the US designation), ambushes, and local community embeddedness — self-defense groups and community police forces provide informal alliances.

## Succession uncertainty
CU is inherently unstable as a coalition. Its members have conflicting local interests. If CJNG pressure decreases (post-Mencho consolidation), CU may fracture as the unifying threat diminishes.
