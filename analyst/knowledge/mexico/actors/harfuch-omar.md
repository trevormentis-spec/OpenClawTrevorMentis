# Actor: Omar García Harfuch

**Role:** Secretary of Security and Citizen Protection (SSPC), Mexico
**Status:** Active — Sheinbaum cabinet, appointed October 2024
**Themes:** cartel_security, political_risk
**Full name:** Omar Hamid García Harfuch

## Who
- Born February 25, 1982, Cuernavaca, Morelos (age 44)
- Morena member since 2023; previously non-partisan security professional
- Former police chief of Mexico City (2019-2023) under then-Mayor Sheinbaum
- Survived a CJNG assassination attempt on June 26, 2020 — ambushed on Paseo de la Reforma, 400+ rounds into armored SUV, killed two bodyguards and a civilian
- **Dubbed "Mexico's Batman"** by LA Times (Nov 2025) after leading multiple high-profile operations
- Clear 2030 presidential frontrunner in internal Morena polling

## Background
- Trained in the Federal Police (Policía Federal) and Criminal Investigation Agency (AIC)
- Advanced coursework at Harvard, FBI Academy (Quantico), DEA
- **Family legacy:** Grandfather Gen. Marcelino García Barragán was Secretary of National Defense (1964-1970), in charge during the 1968 Tlatelolco massacre. Father Javier García Paniagua led the Federal Security Directorate (DFS) during the Dirty War (1976-1978), later headed PRI. Mother is actress/singer María Sorté (María Harfuch Hidalgo) — Lebanese ancestry.
- This family background is controversial — critics cite the repressive legacies; supporters counter he represents a democratic-era generation.
