# Actor: Samuel García Sepúlveda

**Role:** Governor of Nuevo León (Movimiento Ciudadano)
**Status:** Active — second term (elected 2021, re-elected 2024)
**Age:** ~38 (born 1988)
**Theme:** political_risk, economy_markets, worldcup_travel

## Who
Governor of Mexico's industrial powerhouse (Monterrey metro area). Movimiento Ciudadano — centrist party, opposition to Morena. Former presidential candidate (2024, briefly before withdrawing). The only significant opposition-party governor in northern Mexico.

## Why He Matters
- Nuevo León is Mexico's #2 economy (after CDMX) — ~8% of national GDP
- Monterrey hosts World Cup 2026 matches — venue security and tourism
- His state is the primary nearshoring beneficiary (Tesla, manufacturing, KIO data centers)
- He's a potential 2030 presidential candidate for the opposition
- Policy tension with Sheinbaum: infrastructure funding, water rights, border toll disputes
- His governance is Morena's counter-narrative: economic growth 3.1% vs national 1.8% (H1 2024)

## What He Controls
- Nuevo León state government, budget, security apparatus
- Monterrey metro area — industrial and tech hub
- KIO data center cluster oversight
- World Cup 2026 planning for Monterrey venues

## What He Wants
- Maximize nearshoring FDI for Nuevo León
- Secure World Cup infrastructure investment
- Build national profile for 2030 presidential run
- Maintain Nuevo León as a Morena counterweight
