# Actor: Carlos Slim Helú

**Role:** Mexico's wealthiest business magnate
**Age:** 86 (born January 28, 1940)
**Theme:** economy_markets, energy_infra

## Who
Carlos Slim is Mexico's richest person (estimated net worth $60-100B). Controls Grupo Carso (telecoms, infrastructure, mining, retail, energy) and América Móvil (Telmex/Telcel). Built fortune in the 1980s-90s through privatization of state assets under Carlos Salinas de Gortari (Telmex monopoly).

## Why He Matters
- América Móvil controls ~70% of Mexico's mobile market + fixed-line empire
- Grupo Carso is a major Pemex contractor, pipeline builder, infrastructure developer
- Slim's infrastructure investments across Mexico are cartel-extortion targets
- His business sentiment is a leading indicator of Mexico's investment climate
- USMCA 2026 outcomes directly affect Slim's cross-border telecom and trade interests
- Pemex debt exposure: Carso is Pemex's largest private contractor

## What They Want
- Stable regulatory environment (CFE/IFT independence)
- Continued telecom dominance in Mexican market
- Protection of infrastructure investments from cartel extortion
- Favorable treatment in USMCA digital trade provisions
