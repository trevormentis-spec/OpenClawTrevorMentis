# Actor: Edgar Amador Zamora

**Role:** Secretary of Finance and Public Credit (Hacienda)
**Status:** Active — appointed March 2025
**Age:** 58 (born July 23, 1967)
**Theme:** economy_markets

## Who
Mexico's finance minister. Succeeded Rogelio Ramírez de la O (who stepped down in early 2025). Previously undersecretary of finance and public credit under Ramírez. Career civil servant in Hacienda. Background in public finance, not private sector.

## Why He Matters
- Manages Mexico's sovereign debt profile ($105B+ Pemex debt, external debt)
- Sets fiscal policy — budget decisions affect Pemex subsidies, CFE investment, social spending
- Peso and bond market confidence depend on his credibility with international investors
- Recently pushed back against S&P's negative outlook on Mexico — "Mexico is ready to grow"
- Fiscal discipline vs. Morena social spending is the core tension of his role

## Key Watch Items
- S&P/Moody's/Fitch sovereign credit rating actions
- Pemex debt burden management strategy
- Budget deficit trajectory
- Response to any USMCA-related fiscal pressure
- Public signals of independence vs. political direction on spending
