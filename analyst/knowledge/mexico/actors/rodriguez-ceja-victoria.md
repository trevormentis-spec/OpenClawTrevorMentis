# Actor: Victoria Rodríguez Ceja

**Role:** Governor, Bank of Mexico (Banxico)
**Status:** Active — term 2022-2028
**Theme:** economy_markets
**Background:** Economist, former deputy minister at Hacienda

## Who
Central bank governor. First woman to lead Banxico. Appointed by AMLO (2021, confirmed 2022). Career civil servant in finance ministry before Banxico. Technically independent as Banxico governor, but AMLO-appointed.

## Why She Matters
- Sets Mexico's monetary policy: interest rate decisions directly affect peso, inflation, and nearshoring FDI
- Banxico independence has been tested by Morena's supermayoría and judicial reform
- Inflation management defines Mexico investment environment
- Policy rate affects peso carry trade attractiveness
- USMCA review creates economic uncertainty she must navigate

## Key Watch Items
- Banxico rate decisions (every 6 weeks) — spread vs Fed funds rate drives peso
- Public statements on inflation outlook — credibility premium
- Any political pressure signals from Sheinbaum administration
- USMCA/tariff uncertainty impact on Banxico economic projections
